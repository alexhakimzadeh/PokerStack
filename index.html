PokerStack Support

PokerStack is a poker chip calculator designed to help hosts quickly determine the ideal chip distribution for tournaments and cash games.

How PokerStack Works

Enter your buy-in amount, player count, and chip denominations. PokerStack automatically calculates the optimal chip breakdown for your game.
